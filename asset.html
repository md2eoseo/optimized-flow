DXC Business Intelligence for Microsoft

Deliver value and insight with business intelligence

You need to access accurate, relevant business data, and to present it to colleagues in your organization in simple and accessible formats. With DXC Business Intelligence for Microsoft, you can share near real time operational information from across your business in visually appealing formats to drive daily decisions and strategic planning.

Advantages

Open and scaleable BI solution for Microsoft Dynamics AX and Dynamics 365 for Finance and Operations
Requires only the software you potentially already have,* or with your preferred third party BI solution
Set up in just a few days
Provide common best practice reports and dashboards
Customized to meet your changing needs
Use as a standalone solution, or integrated with your Dynamics solution and Microsoft Power BI**

Optimize your business management baseline

Business intelligence is about ensuring that your company’s employees and management teams have the information they need to do their jobs effectively. DXC Business Intelligence for Microsoft is a simple and accessible solution that helps ensure that you always have access to current, accurate and relevant information that can be used as a shared baseline on which to base informed business decisions, and define strategic direction. You can integrate our solution with Microsoft Power BI to provide operational status on, for example, monitors in your production facility or warehouse areas, or you can embed a dashboard into Microsoft Dynamics AX.

Flexible reporting out of the box

The solution’s predefined data model can cover many of your reporting needs out of the box, and adding additional data needs is simple. You can tailor the provided reports to your individual user groups, and provide both flexible self service capabilities and a robust common corporate baseline.

Deliver valuable, timely information and insight

Based on a common baseline, you can provide your management with strategic overviews and KPIs and, at the same time, provide your operational staff with actionable near real time data on:

  • Procurement
  • Sales
  • Project
  • Inventory
  • Production
  • Finance
  • Retail
  • Human resources

DXC Technology’s data model structures your Dynamics AX or Dynamics 365 for Finance and Operations data and makes it easily accessible in a business intelligence solution. The result is a simple but powerful stand alone business intelligence implementation. All output is based on a common data warehouse and data model and is available as both self service and corporate cube based Microsoft Excel reports and Power BI dashboards. KPIs can be displayed in company portals and key figures can be shown embedded in Dynamics AX in both Role Centers as well as forms of your choice.

Features

Easily customize and modify the solution as your requirements change
Fast and easy setup, with eight standardized analysis sets for finance, procurement, sales, project, inventory, production, retail and human resources
Present data as reports in Excel, as dashboards in Power BI, or in your preferred presentation tool
Generate self service analysis in Excel with PowerPivot and Microsoft Power BI dashboards
Generate corporate reports based on multidimensional or in memory cubes
Integrate historical data from other systems or a previous Dynamics AX solution
Show key figures, report and dashboard links directly within Dynamics AX

For large and small companies

The solution is extremely scalable and flexible, and can generate business value for small, midsize and large organizations. It can also grow with you as your business thrives and your needs change.

Get up and running fast

Typical business intelligence solutions can take months to configure and implement. DXC Business Intelligence for Microsoft can be installed quickly and easily in just a few days.

Prerequisites:

Microsoft Office 2013 or Microsoft Office 2016 and Microsoft Power BI Desktop. Microsoft SQL Server 2008 R2 or Microsoft SQL Server 2012/2014/2016 Microsoft Dynamics AX 2009SP1, 2012R2, 2012R3, Dynamics 365 for Finance and Operations
